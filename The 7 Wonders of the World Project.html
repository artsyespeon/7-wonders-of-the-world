The 7 Wonders of the World

In early 2000, a poll was opened for anyone who wished to participate in voting for their top destinations (from a list of over 200). From this list the Seven Wonders of the Modern-Day World were revealed. Theese 7 Wonders are:

  • The Petra (Jordan)
  • The Machu Picchu (Peru)
  • The Taj Mahal (Agra, India)
  • The Roman Colosseum (Rome)
  • The Chichén Itzá (Mexico)
  • The Christ the Redeemer Statue (Brazil)
  • The Great Wall of China (China)
